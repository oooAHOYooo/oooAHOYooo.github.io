    About Ahoy Indie Media

    Ahoy Indie Media is a hand-crafted media company based out of New Haven, Connecticut. Our mission is to provide a homegrown alternative to the way folks consume media. We believe in good media - good indie media - the real stuff.

    Our platform is dedicated to supporting independent artists, musicians, podcasters, and creators by providing them with a space to share their work and connect with audiences.

    We sustain our operations through a surcharge on merchandise sold through our store and through monthly membership donations. This model allows us to support our artists while keeping the platform accessible and ad-free.

    Every piece of music on our platform is here with the express permission of the artists involved.

    Burn a CD : Save the World

    Support Local Musicians - foster new industry reality

    How it works:

    • 1. Select the songs you want to include in your CD. You can choose up to 15 songs or up to 70 minutes total.
    • 2. Rearrange your playlist by dragging the songs into your preferred order.
    • 3. Give your CD a unique name.
    • 4. Review your playlist. You can remove songs or add more as long as you stay within the limit.
    • 5. Submit your CD burn request. Rob Meglio will personally curate your playlist, write a personalized note, and design the CD cover with a sharpie.
    • 6. Your CD will be mailed to your house within 2-4 weeks. You will be contacted for payment and shipping details shortly.
