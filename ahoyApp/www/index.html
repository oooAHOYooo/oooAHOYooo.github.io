              This feature combines a Pomodoro-style timer with a classical music playlist to enhance focus and productivity. Users can start a 25-minute work session using the timer, which displays remaining time and a circular progress indicator. Simultaneously, they can enjoy a curated classical music playlist, with options to play individual tracks, shuffle the playlist, or play all songs sequentially. The timer alerts users when the work session ends, while the music continues playing, automatically moving to the next track. This combination of timed work intervals and soothing classical music aims to create an ideal environment for concentrated work or study.
