    About Ahoy Indie Media

    Ahoy Indie Media is a hand-crafted media company based out of New Haven, Connecticut. Our mission is to provide a homegrown alternative to the way folks consume media. We believe in good media - good indie media - the real stuff.

    Our platform is dedicated to supporting independent artists, musicians, podcasters, and creators by providing them with a space to share their work and connect with audiences.

    We sustain our operations through sheer will power. This model allows us to support our artists while keeping the platform accessible and ad-free.

    Every piece of content on our platform is here with the express blessings of the artists involved.

    How You Can Get Involved

    • Share a Song or Media: Have a song or media that fits Ahoy? Share it with us for a chance to be featured.
    • Review New Releases: Keep an eye out for new releases and share your thoughts. Coming soon!
    • Donate: Support our mission and help keep the indie spirit alive and for the cause - we're here to stay.
    • Spread the Word: Help us grow by sharing Ahoy - word of mouth is our most powerful weapon agaisnt the wild marketing robots
    • Help Market Ahoy: Assist in marketing efforts to help Ahoy reach a wider audience.
