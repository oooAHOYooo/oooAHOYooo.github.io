Ahoy Indie Media

Ahoy Indie Media is a New Haven streaming platform for independent music, podcasts, events, and films. It is built to feel local, ad-free, and direct, with support flowing back to creators and communities.

How it works

The site should be easy to understand in review and easy to use on a phone. These steps cover the basics of the app experience.

1
Create an account

Open the app, choose Sign Up, and follow the prompts to get started.

2
Explore local media

Browse music, podcasts, live events, and video from New Haven and nearby creators.

3
Use the wallet to support creators

The wallet is used for in-app support and purchases so fans can boost artists directly.

4
Find shows fast

Use Events or the New Haven page to see upcoming shows, venues, and schedules.
