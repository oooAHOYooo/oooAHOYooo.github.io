Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Ahoy Indie Media handles information across the website and related app services. It is written to be straightforward and review-friendly.

What we collect

Information you provide

We may collect information you send us directly, such as account details, support messages, creator submissions, and any information you enter while using the app or website.

Automatic information

We may automatically receive limited technical data such as IP address, device or browser details, and basic usage logs so the service can run, stay secure, and improve over time.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the website and app.
  • Respond to support requests and account questions.
  • Understand usage patterns and improve the experience.
  • Protect the service from abuse, fraud, or security issues.

Sharing and retention

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We may share data with service providers that help us host, operate, or support the product.
  • We keep information only as long as needed for the reasons above or as required by law.

Your choices

You can contact us to ask about your information, correct a problem, or request help with account-related issues. If you no longer want to use the service, email us and we will help where we can.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, or follow-up on a request, email contact@littlemarket.org.
