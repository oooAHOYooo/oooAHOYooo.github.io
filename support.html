FAQ

Here are the common questions we expect people to ask during review and day-to-day use.

How do I create an account?

Open the app, choose Sign Up, and follow the prompts. If anything gets stuck, email contact@littlemarket.org.

How does the wallet work?

The wallet is the in-app balance used for support and purchases so fans can boost creators directly.

How do I find local shows?

Use the Events section in the app or the New Haven page to browse upcoming shows, venues, and schedules.

How do I contact you?

Email contact@littlemarket.org for support or general questions.

Where is the privacy policy?

The current policy lives at ahoy.ooo/privacy.
